Warm up

Duration: 15 minutes

Structure: 3 sets of 5 minutes each

Includes: Dynamic stretches (arm swings, leg swings), light cardio (jogging in place, jumping jacks)

Benefits: Increases heart rate and circulation, prepares muscles for intense activity, enhances flexibility, and reduces injury risk

Estimated calorie burn: 100 calories

Arm Workout

Duration: 20 minutes

Structure: 4 sets of 5 minutes each

Includes: Bicep curls, tricep dips, shoulder press

Benefits: Strengthens arm muscles, enhances endurance

Estimated calorie burn: 150 calories
